Penguin Sushi


Image Source

Penguins have flippers. This is the main reason they don't make sushi.

Luckily, you can help.

Ingredients

  • 1 Penguin (up to 10 lbs, adult chinstrap)
  • 1/2 Lb Sushi-Grade Raw Salmon
  • 2 Cups Sushi Rice, Prepared
  • 6 Sheets Nori
  • 4 Oz Cream Cheese
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Bamboo Mat

Directions

  1. Place penguin on countertop
  2. Place seaweed on mat and cover with an even layer of rice
  3. Layer salmon onto the rice
  4. Restrain the penguin
  5. Add cream cheese and avocado
  6. Roll tightly (the sushi, not the penguin)
  7. Unleash the penguin. That's a good penguin!
