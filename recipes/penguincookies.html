Penguin Cookies


Image Source

Sometimes penguins dream of cookies.

When they do, they dream of these cookies:

Ingredients

  • 1 Penguin (up to 10 lbs, adult chinstrap)
  • 2 Fish
  • 1 Cup Butter, Softened
  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Tsp Baking Sodea
  • 3 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Cups Semisweet Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Place penguin on countertop and give one fish
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  3. Mix brown and white sugar, salt, and baking soda, with butter until smooth
  4. Stir in chocolate chips
  5. Add by the spoonful to ungreased pans
  6. Bake for 10 minutes
  7. Give penguin second fish for a job well done. That's a good penguin!
