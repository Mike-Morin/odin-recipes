Penguin Cookies


Image Source

Sometimes penguins dream of cookies.

When they do, they dream of these cookies:

Ingredients

  • 1 Penguin (up to 10 lbs, adult chinstrap)
  • 2 Fish
  • 1 Cup Butter, Softened
  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Tsp Baking Sodea
  • 2 Tsp Water
  • 3 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Cups Semisweet Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Place penguin on countertop and give one fish
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  3. Mix brown sugar, white sugar, and butter until smooth
  4. Stir in eggs and vanilla
  5. Dissolve salt and baking soda in water and stir into batter
  6. Stir in chocolate chips
  7. Add dough by the spoonful to ungreased pans
  8. Bake for 10 minutes
  9. Give penguin second fish for a job well done. That's a good penguin!
